Summary

"Super 30" is a biographical drama based on the life of Anand Kumar, a mathematician from Bihar who trains underprivileged students for the IIT entrance exams. The film showcases his struggles against the system and his dedication to empowering the poor through education.

Actors

  • Hrithik Roshan as Anand Kumar
  • Mrunal Thakur as Supriya
  • Pankaj Tripathi as Devraj Jagan Safdurjang
  • Virendra Saxena as Rajendra Kumar
